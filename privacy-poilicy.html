Welcome to Prime Work Solutions

(“we,” “our,” or “us”). Your privacy and data protection are extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website, mobile app, or any of our services (collectively, the “Platform”).

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information (Job Seekers)

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and address
  • Date of birth
  • National ID (NID) number and scanned copy
  • Passport number and scanned copy
  • CV/resume, employment history, skills, education, and certifications
  • Profile photo or verification documents

Company Information (Employers)

  • Company name and contact details
  • Business address
  • Trade license or registration details
  • Job postings and recruitment data

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address, browser type, and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited, session duration, and usage behavior

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Verify identities and prevent fraud
  • Match job seekers with verified employers
  • Share resumes with companies to help you find suitable jobs
  • Allow employers to contact you for job offers
  • Send job alerts, updates, and notifications
  • Improve our Platform and user experience
  • Ensure compliance with Bangladesh and international recruiting, immigration, and data protection laws

3. Storage and Security of Sensitive Documents

Your NID and Passport details are collected solely for identity verification and legal compliance.

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure servers with restricted access
  • Access limited to authorized personnel only

We never publish or share your passport or NID data without your explicit consent, except when required by law.

4. Resume Information Sharing

By uploading or creating your CV/resume, you consent to us sharing it with:

  • Verified employers and companies registered on Prime Work Solutions
  • Partner agencies or recruiters under legal and contractual agreements

You can control your resume visibility or delete it anytime via your account settings.

5. No Personal Deal Policy

  • All recruitment and communication must occur through our official channels only.
  • Job seekers should never pay individuals or third parties for employment offers.
  • Employers and agencies must not request or offer money or gifts to influence hiring decisions.
  • Violations may result in account suspension or legal action.

We promote fair, transparent, and lawful recruitment practices in compliance with Bangladesh laws and international labor standards.

Report Suspicious Activity: support@primeworksolutions.com

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for recruitment, verification, or legal purposes. You may request account or data deletion by contacting us directly.

7. Legal Compliance

We operate in compliance with:

Bangladesh Laws:

  • Overseas Employment and Migrants Act 2013
  • Overseas Employment and Migrants (Amendment) Act 2023
  • Emigration Ordinance 1982 and related rules

International Laws:

  • GDPR (EU)
  • CCPA (California, USA)
  • Other applicable international privacy and labor laws

We only share your data with licensed recruiting agencies, government bodies, or employers for lawful purposes such as visa processing or employment verification.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our Platform is not intended for individuals under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Any data found from a minor will be deleted immediately.

9. Cookies

  • Improve browsing experience
  • Analyze traffic and user behavior
  • Remember preferences

You can manage or disable cookies via your browser settings.

10. Your Rights

  • Access and review your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

11. Third-Party Links

Our Platform may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy policies or data practices.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on our Platform with a new “Last Updated” date.

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or complaints, please contact us at support@primeworksolutions.com.
